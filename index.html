Power is a System, Not a Component.

We don't build dumb power supplies. We build engineered power infrastructure. In our ecosystem, power has state, telemetry, and explicit safety boundaries.

Every module is electrically independent, constantly communicating, and fully aware of the system state.

Non-Negotiable Philosophy

Hardware First

Software may configure and visualize, but it never overrides safety. All limits are defined and enforced in hardware.

System Telemetry

Visibility is not optional. Every module reports voltage, current, and fault states to a central aggregator in real-time.

Modularity

A plug-and-play ecosystem where modules can be combined arbitrarily. The system adapts automatically to new configurations.
