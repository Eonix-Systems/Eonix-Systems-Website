Founder Story

Eonix Systems was founded by Rishabh Jain, an electronics engineering student and hardware developer focused on embedded systems, PCB design, power electronics, and robotics hardware.

The company started from a simple observation: many hardware projects fail not because the idea is weak, but because the electronics are built in fragments. Power is handled separately, firmware is patched later, sensors are added without proper communication planning, and debugging becomes chaotic.

Through robotics projects, PCB design work, firmware experiments, and system-level hardware development, Eonix was built to solve this gap.

Today, Eonix Systems focuses on helping teams build reliable electronic systems - from schematic and PCB layout to firmware, bring-up, testing, and manufacturing support.

Our goal is simple: help builders move from messy prototypes to reliable working hardware.
