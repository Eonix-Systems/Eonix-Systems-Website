Why Work With Eonix

Eonix is built for practical embedded electronics work: circuits that can be assembled, firmware that can be tested, and handover notes another engineer can use.

Capability and Trust

We Understand the Full Embedded Stack

PCB design, firmware, power, communication, and bring-up are connected. Treating them separately often creates avoidable problems.

Practical Over Flashy

We focus on circuits that can be assembled, firmware that can be tested, and documentation that another engineer can actually use.

Built for Early-Stage Hardware Teams

Many teams do not need a large engineering department immediately. They need focused help to move from idea, partial prototype, or broken board to a working hardware revision.

Close to the Bench

Founder Note

Eonix Systems was started by Rishabh Jain with a focus on embedded electronics, PCB design, and hardware prototyping.

The company was built from hands-on engineering work: designing boards, debugging firmware, testing circuits, and solving the kind of practical issues that appear only when hardware is actually built.
