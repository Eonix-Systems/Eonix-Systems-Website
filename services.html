SYS_SVC_02 // DETAIL

Service Areas

Each area can be handled independently or connected into a full hardware development workflow.

PCB Design Services

Board design support from schematic structure to production-ready fabrication and assembly outputs.

Best suited for: teams that need a new board, a board revision, or a manufacturable PCB from an existing concept.

  • Schematic design
  • PCB layout
  • 2-layer and 4-layer boards
  • Component selection
  • Power routing
  • Design rule checks
  • Manufacturing files
  • BOM
  • Pick-and-place
  • Assembly notes

Embedded Firmware Development

Firmware development for board-level control, sensor communication, telemetry, and reliable device behavior.

Best suited for: prototypes and embedded products that need stable control logic and hardware-aware firmware.

  • STM32 firmware
  • ESP32 firmware
  • Sensor interfacing
  • CAN, UART, SPI, I2C
  • BLE/WiFi integration where needed
  • Data logging and telemetry
  • Control logic
  • Debugging and testing

Hardware Bring-up & Debugging

Structured validation after assembly, focused on finding electrical, firmware, and integration faults early.

Best suited for: boards that have been fabricated, assembled, or partially tested and are not behaving correctly.

  • First power-up checks
  • Voltage rail validation
  • Current consumption checks
  • Oscilloscope and multimeter debugging
  • Firmware-hardware integration
  • Fault diagnosis
  • Design revision recommendations

Prototype to Small-Batch Support

Practical preparation for moving from a working prototype into repeatable assembly and testing.

Best suited for: teams preparing a prototype for hand assembly, vendor assembly, or a small production run.

  • Manufacturing preparation
  • BOM cleanup
  • Assembly planning
  • Stencil and soldering guidance
  • Testing jig planning
  • Documentation handover

Power Electronics & Protection

Power path, switching, distribution, protection, and thermal decisions for embedded and robotic hardware.

Best suited for: projects with motors, batteries, converters, high-current rails, noisy loads, or sensitive electronics.

  • Buck converter design
  • Power distribution
  • MOSFET switching
  • Reverse polarity protection
  • Overcurrent and short-circuit protection
  • TVS, fuse, and protection selection
  • Thermal and layout considerations
